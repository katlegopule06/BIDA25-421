About Havre du Macramé

Havre Du Macramé is an online space about a form of textile art which includes knotting strings to create beautiful crafts using hands and supporters. Macrame originates from the 13th Century Arabic weavers evolving from practical knots by sailors to Victorian-era luxury decoration and experiencing major resurgences in the 1970s and modern Bohemian home decor. Source: Domestika

VISION: Inspire creativity and passion for Macramé around the world.

MISSION: Provide beautiful macrame products, helpful learning resources and a supportive creative community.
