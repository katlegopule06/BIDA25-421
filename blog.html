What is Macramé?

Macramé includes various knot types like berry knots, lark’s head knot, square knot, spiral knot,double half hitch knot,josephine knot etc.

Macramé Care Tips

  • Gently dust the artwork regularly using a cloth.
  • Keep the artwork away from moist areas.
  • Store in shaded areas.
  • Avoid folding to prevent creases.
  • Handle carefully to avoid fraying.

Macramé Decoration Ideas

  • Wall hangings are the most suitable for living rooms, dining rooms and bedrooms.
  • Plant hangers work best near windows and doors.
  • Boho home decor can be used on cushion covers as well as shelves.
  • Entry way styling works best hanged by entrances that do not necessarily need doors like dining rooms.
  • Accessories like handbags pair well with charms and used for everyday life duties, clothes like tops, dresses and cardigans work too for aesthetics.
